About OPSS - K

Welcome to Orpcare Primary and Secondary School. We offer a value-based, child-centered curriculum that supports the holistic development of every child. Orpcare Secondary School is a reputable educational institution located in Kayonza District, Eastern Province, Rwanda. Established in 2008 by Gakwaya Leonard, the school began with humble beginnings, initially renting classrooms before constructing its own facilities. Over the years, it has expanded to offer education from nursery through to secondary levels, including day and boarding options.
